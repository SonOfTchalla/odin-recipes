Pizza Recipe

Pepperoni Pizza

This is a quick and easy recipes to make peperoni pizza at home.

Ingredients

  • Store bought pizza dough
  • Pepperoni sausages
  • A block of Feta cheese
  • Green Chillies
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Roll the pizza dough thin but not too thin. then cut out a circle shape, size depending on your preference/baking tray.
  2. Spread two table spoons of Tomato paste over the dough evenly.
  3. Cube the cheese block and add on top of the pizza generously
  4. Slice the peperoni sausages thinly and spread on top of the cheese.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius and leave in for 20-30 minutes
